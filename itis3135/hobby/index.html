What is this hobby about?

About Press-On Nails

Creating press-on nails is a hands-on, creative hobby that involves designing and crafting custom nail sets tailored to individual styles and preferences. It allows for endless customization, from choosing specific sizes, shapes, and lengths to painting intricate designs and adding decorations like gems or charms. The process is both artistic and practical: after designing, the nails are simply popped onto natural nails using adhesive, giving a salon-quality look without the commitment or expense of professional manicures. This hobby combines the fun of DIY with the flexibility to switch up styles easily, making it perfect for self-expression.

How long do press on nails last?

Depends on what method you use to adhere them!

There are two different ways to adhere press-on nails:

  • Nail Glue - Glue is a strong adhesive that holds the nails in place for a longer period of time. It can last up to 2-3 weeks if proper nail prep is done.
  • Gel Tabs - Gel tabs are nail shaped stickers that hold the nails in place. This method will last 1-2 days and is a good option for special occasions or low commitment clients.

Where do I start?

Beginner Nail Art Workspace

Start by creating your own nail art workspace at home.

First start by sourcing supplies, such as:

  • UV Lamp - This will be used to cure gel polishes used on the nails.
  • Brushes - I recommend starting with small, find detailed brushes. This will make nail art much easier.
  • Glue or Gel Tabs - Do your research on the best brands for your personal needs.
  • Full Length Nail Tips - Thee are many different brands and styles of nail tips, choose your favorite shape and length.
  • Nail Art Tools - I reccomend starting with your basics, such as:
    • Nail File and Nail Buff
    • Cuticle Pusher and Nippers
    • Lint-Free Wipes
    • Acetone and Rubbing Alcohol
    • Gloves and Mask
    • Optional - Cuticle Oil
  • A Press-On Nail stand - This is what you will attach your nail tips to while you work on your nails.

How

Creating Press-On Nails

  • Select your Size and Shape:
    • Start by measuring your natural nails to determine the correct size.
    • Choose your desired shape and length.
  • Prepare Your Workstation:
    • Gather all necessary materials: nail tips, glue tabs or adhesion clay, nail polish, design tools, and a stand for the nails.
  • Adhere Nails to the Stand:
    • Use glue tabs or adhesion clay to secure your nails to the stand. This makes it easier to hold the nails while painting and designing.
  • Paint and Design:
    • Apply a base coat followed by your chosen nail polish color.
    • Be sure to cure between each coat per product instructions.
    • Get creative! Use nail art tools for designs like nail stickers, gems, or freehand art.
  • Final Touches:
    • Apply your favorite top coat for shine and durability.
    • Once you're satisfied with your design, gently pop the nails off the stand.
    • Your press-on nails are now ready to be applied to your natural nails!

Why should you get into this hobby?

Reasons to Get Into the Hobby of Creating Your Own Press-On Nails

  • Creativity: Designing your own press-on nails allows you to express your artistic side and create unique looks tailored to your style.
  • Cost-Effective: Making your own nails can save you money compared to regular salon visits. You can invest in materials and create multiple sets over time.
  • Customization: You can choose colors, shapes, and designs that reflect your personality.
  • Convenience: Press-on nails are easy to apply and remove, making them perfect for anyone with a busy lifestyle who still wants to feel beautiful.
  • Skill Development: This hobby helps you improve your crafting skills, particularly in areas like painting, sculpting, and design.
  • Community: Join communities or groups of fellow nail enthusiasts to share tips, designs, and support each other in your nail art journeys.
