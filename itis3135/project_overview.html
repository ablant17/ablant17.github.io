Jordan Qualls' Portfolio

Project Overview

This project aims to create a portfolio website for Jordan Qualls, showcasing her work in nails, press-on nails, and makeup. The website will serve as an online portfolio for her services and include various interactive features to engage potential clients and display her professional achievements.

Intended Users

  • Potential clients interested in Jordan’s nail and makeup services.
  • General visitors looking for beauty inspiration.
  • Partners or collaborators interested in her work and publications.

Overview of Content

  • Home Page: Introduction and overview of services.
  • About Page: Background information about Jordan.
  • Portfolio Page: Gallery of images showcasing her work.
  • Contact Page: Links to her social media accounts.
  • Publications Page A collection of articles and blog posts.

Site Map

Page Designs

Home Page (index.html)
  • Purpose: To introduce visitors to Jordan Qualls and showcase her portfolio.
  • Audience/Users: Potential clients, customers, or fans interested in nails, press-on nails, and makeup.
  • Content: Her logo and name at the top of the page, one large image (of her choosing), and links to her social media at the bottom of the page.
  • Data Entry: None.
  • Validations: None.
  • Buttons/Hyperlinks: Navigation links to "About," "Portfolio," "Publications," and "Contact" pages as well as social media links
  • Actions: Clicking on navigation links directs the user to other pages.
  • Special Notes: Make this page visually appealing as it is the landing page.
About Page (about.html)
  • Purpose: To provide a biography and background information about Jordan Qualls.
  • Audience/Users: Clients and visitors who want to know more about Jordan’s experience.
  • Content: Jordan's bio, her journey in makeup and nails, and why she’s passionate about her work.
  • Data Entry: None.
  • Validations: None.
  • Buttons/Hyperlinks: Navigation links to other pages.
  • Actions: Clicking on navigation links directs the user to other sections of the website.
  • Special Notes: None.
Portfolio Page (portfolio.html)
  • Purpose: To display Jordan’s work, including nails, press-on nails, and makeup projects.
  • Audience/Users: Clients interested in Jordan's services and portfolio.
  • Content: Gallery of images showcasing Jordan's work, descriptions of each project, and possibly videos.
  • Data Entry: No data entry required.
  • Validations: Not applicable.
  • Buttons/Hyperlinks: Links for each portfolio category (nails, makeup, sfx-makeup).
  • Actions: Clicking on a portfolio will show a dropdown menu of different types of projects.
  • Special Notes: Ensure images are well-organized and responsive.
Publications Page (publications.html)
  • Purpose: To list articles, features, or blog posts where Jordan's work has been showcased.
  • Audience/Users: Clients and fans looking for more in-depth information about Jordan’s work in the media.
  • Content: A list of publications, articles, or press mentions, with brief descriptions and links to the original sources.
  • Data Entry: No data entry required.
  • Validations: Not applicable.
  • Buttons/Hyperlinks: Links to external articles or blogs.
  • Actions: Clicking on a link opens the publication in a new tab.
  • Special Notes: Open links in new tabs to avoid losing the visitor.
Contact Page (contact.html)
  • Purpose: To allow visitors to contact Jordan Qualls for inquiries or bookings.
  • Audience/Users: Potential clients who wish to contact Jordan for her services.
  • Content: This page will include all of her contact methods, including email, phone, and social media.
  • Data Entry: None.
  • Validations: None.
  • Buttons/Hyperlinks: Links to her social media accounts.
  • Actions:Clicking on social media links will bring user to her profiles.
  • Special Notes:Open links in new tabs to avoid losing the user.

Dynamic Functionality

The website will include the following interactive features:

  • Portfolio Page: A gallery using the Lightbox plugin for viewing images.
  • Contact Page: Hyperlinks to social media accounts
  • Navigation Bar: A fixed navigation bar that scrolls with the page.
  • Dynamic Filtering: The portfolio page will include filters to switch between categories of work.
